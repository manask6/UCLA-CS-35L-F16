The fstat, mmap, read, open, close, and mprotect calls seem to be the ones
that relate to dynamic linking. Fstat calculates the size of those libraries,
mmap makes the map between the libraries and memory, read reads data from
the ELF object, open opens the libraries, close closes them, and mprotect
protects the library code from malicious attacks.
